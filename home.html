Women in Engineering VEX-U Robotics Team

VEX-U

VEX-U is a project team maintained by the Women in Engineering Department at Texas A&M University. We are an all-female team from various engineering disciplines and backgrounds. The team has students from freshman year to senior year, allowing all levels of skill to participate. The focus of our project team is to allow new engineers to grow their skill set through practice and competition. We accept new members year-round, so come on out to see what we are all about!
